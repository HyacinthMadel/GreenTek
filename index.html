About Climate Action Climate action is the effort to protect our planet from the impacts of climate change and create a sustainable future for everyone. It involves three important steps: monitoring the environment to understand problems like pollution and deforestation, reducing harmful activities by using clean energy, conserving water, and minimizing waste, and empowering people and communities with the knowledge, tools, and resources they need to make positive changes.
